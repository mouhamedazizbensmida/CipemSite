REMOVE THE NOISE IN YOUR CABLES


How Do Elesol Plates Work?


ELESOL can reduce electricity consumption by sticking it to a circuit breaker or cable. The explanation of the mechanism is below:

1

The power supply of factories and buildings must be alternating and sinusoidal

direct current = constant flow.

2

Circuit breakers and inverters are sources of current noise. These "noises" or "harmonics" circulate with the wave of electric current.

3

You can suppress the noise with “filter effect” by sticking the ELESOL plate after the circuit breaker. By affixing the ELESOL plate, the electric current will be cleaned.

4

The more noise there is, the more electromagnetic waves are wasted in the air. A huge amount of electricity is wasted when electromagnetic waves are emitted into the air. Example: radio waves (Wi-Fi, BT Waves, etc.)

5

By eliminating noise, we begin to remove the unnecessary consumption of electricity that we pay for but don't use.
