MAINTENANCE ENGINEERING

Our maintenance engineering services allow CIPEM to meet the specific needs of its customers and help us develop and improve the management of their assets. We offer technical solutions to reduce costs and increase equipment availability, providing the highest standards in terms of reliability and productivity for maintenance systems.

EXPERTISE

The objective of the expertise activity is focused on the definition of a visibility of the state of the installation making it possible to identify the direct and indirect causes in order to develop the main preventive and / or corrective maintenance actions for the failures.

ENERGY SAVING & ENERGY EFFICIENCY

We work on issues of production, transmission and distribution of electricity in tertiary and industrial environments. Our sectoral and project approach gives us the ability to develop an adequate, profitable and optimal Energy Efficiency strategy.

CONSULTING

Whether the project is still at the level of intention, idea, design or operational, we can always offer our know-how and our relational capital to provide advice and support to our clients in order to effectively contribute to the success of their business.

ENERGY PHOTOVOLTAIC

Photovoltaic solar energy (or photovoltaic energy or EPV) is electrical energy produced from solar radiation using sensors or photovoltaic solar power plants.
